This is your authority to change the filter tow
specifications on WINSTON King Size cigarettes. The
regular Eastman tow 3.3/42,000 will change to Eastman
tow 3.3/39,000.

Due to the inventory position you may use 3.3/39,000 tow
and 3.3/42,000 tow at the same time. However, they
should not be mixed on the same packing machine.
